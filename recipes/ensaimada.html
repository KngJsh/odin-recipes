Ensaïmada

Ensaimada de Mallorca - original recipe

Ingredients

  • 200ml milk
  • 500gr flour
  • 2 teaspoons dry yeast
  • 100gr sugar
  • a pinch of salt
  • 2 eggs (whisked)
  • 200gr butter (soft, not melted)
  • powdered sugar

Steps

  1. heatup milk
  2. make dough with flour, dry yeast, sugar, salt, eggs and warmed up milk
  3. let dough rest for 1-1.5 hours
  4. knead again for some minutes and roll out to a 50x50cm square
  5. put on butter on top
  6. roll like a snail and put in to the fridge for 15min
  7. take dough out and let it rest for another hour
  8. put in to the oven for 30mins (180°C two-sided heat)
